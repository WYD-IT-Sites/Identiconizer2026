# Privacy Policy — Identiconizer-Reborn

**Last updated:** 2026-03-10

Identiconizer-Reborn is a local-first Android app that generates identicon avatars for contacts on your device.

## Data we access
- **Contacts data** (including contact names and photos) is accessed only to:
  - generate identicons,
  - write generated images as contact photos,
  - remove generated identicons when requested by the user.

## Why permissions are required
- `READ_CONTACTS`: needed to read contacts so identicons can be generated and managed.
- `WRITE_CONTACTS`: needed to apply or remove generated identicon photos.

## Data collection and sharing
- We **do not collect** personal data to remote servers.
- We **do not sell** personal data.
- We **do not share** contacts data with third parties.
- The app does **not** include ads, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

## Data retention and deletion
- Contact data is handled on-device as part of normal app function.
- The app does not maintain a remote user database.
- You can remove generated identicons at any time using in-app controls.

## Security
- Processing is performed locally on-device.
- No cloud account is required to use core identicon features.
